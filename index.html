Hexagon Path

A strategic path-building game on a hexagonal grid.

  • Objective: Be the last survivor or cover the greatest distance.
  • Extend: Play tiles at your current location to extend your path.
  • Navigate: Avoid hitting the outer rim or other players (if collision is on).
  • Controls: Select a tile in your Hand, rotate it, and click "Play Tile".

Adjust settings in the Left Panel and click Start Game to begin.

Music by: kornevmusic, starostin, freemusiclab, miromaxmusic, magpiemusic, lightbeatsmusic, alexzavesa. Source: pixabay.com.
